THE PROJECT

The Valerio Evangelisti Archive is a research project dedicated to the preservation and valorization of the literary heritage of one of Italy's most important science fiction and historical fiction authors.

Through systematic digitization of manuscripts, correspondence, drafts, and unpublished materials, the project aims to create a complete and accessible digital archive for scholars, researchers, and enthusiasts of Evangelisti's work.

The archive includes the author's entire corpus, with particular attention to the celebrated cycle of inquisitor Nicolas Eymerich, enabling in-depth philological studies and analysis of the evolution of his narrative poetics.

The project employs advanced digitization technologies and metadata standards to ensure long-term preservation of original materials, many of which are fragile and require special conservation care.

Through an interactive web platform, designed for both researchers and the wider public, users will be able to navigate through the author's entire production, exploring thematic connections, stylistic evolutions, and the historical-cultural context that influenced his writing.

Methodology

Data was extracted directly from file metadata of the digital archive, analyzing creation and modification dates to identify temporal patterns and editorial correlations.
